# Helpmate 2.0.0: AI CRM & Automation System for WordPress

## Overview

Helpmate 2.0.0 transforms from a support-focused plugin into a complete AI CRM and automation system built for any WordPress business. It centralizes customer communication, CRM, and marketing automation inside your WordPress dashboard.

## Core Features

### AI CRM for WordPress
- 50 contacts free, unlimited in Pro
- Contact profile with structured tabs: Overview, Emails, Tasks, Notes, Conversations, Orders
- WooCommerce order metabox showing CRM contact data
- Segments with condition builder (Pro)
- Custom fields and schedules (Pro)

### Unified Inbox
- AI Chatbot conversations (200 chat credits free)
- Tickets, Live Chat, Social Messages, Social Comments
- Per-type notification counts and in-app notifications
- Business hours support

### AI Chatbot Improvements
- Enhanced AI reasoning capabilities
- Refined data source tabs: File, Post, Products, QnA, Text, URL
- Post and page meta box for training knowledge base
- Behavior settings for tone and language control

### Marketing Automation
- Email Templates with editor and library
- Bulk Email Campaigns and Email Sequences
- Failed email tracking and unsubscribe page
- Conversion tools: Promo Bar, Sales Notifications, Coupon Delivery

### Social Media Integration
- Facebook Messages and Comments (Pro)
- Instagram Messages and Comments (Pro)
- WhatsApp (Pro)
- Social lead campaign builder

### Task Management & Scheduling
- Kanban board and list view
- Appointments and booking system
- Public scheduling page
- Smart scheduling module

### Analytics & Team Management
- CRM Analytics reports for Contacts, Leads, Emails, Tasks
- Team performance tracking
- Five predefined roles with granular permission controls

## Why It Matters

Helpmate 2.0.0 eliminates fragmented tools by consolidating AI Chatbot, CRM, Unified Inbox, Marketing Automation, Conversion Tools, Analytics, and Scheduling into one WordPress dashboard. This replaces multiple separate subscriptions with a unified system.

## Conclusion

Helpmate 2.0.0 positions WordPress as a growth operating system rather than just a content management system. It centralizes customer lifecycle management for WooCommerce stores and serves as a lead, booking, and automation hub for service-based WordPress businesses.

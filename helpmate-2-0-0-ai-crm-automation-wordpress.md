Helpmate 2.0.0 transforms from a support-focused plugin into a full AI CRM and automation system built for WordPress businesses—replacing fragmented tools with a unified platform inside the WordPress dashboard.

## What Changed

### Redesigned Admin Interface
- Icon-based sidebar with submenu panel navigation
- Mobile-responsive sheet-style sidebar
- Notification bell with unread counts and browser tab title sync
- Real-time updates and granular role-based permission system
- New onboarding: 3-step setup wizard (API Key, OpenAI Pro, Data Training), getting started checklist, Free vs Pro comparison table

### AI CRM for WordPress
- 50 contacts free, unlimited in Pro
- Contact profiles with structured tabs: Overview, Emails, Tasks, Notes, Conversations, Orders (WooCommerce), Tickets, Abandoned Carts (Pro), Schedules (Pro)
- Custom fields and segment builder with condition builder (Pro)
- WooCommerce order metabox showing CRM contact data
- Every order automatically links to a contact; support tickets and abandoned carts become part of a unified customer timeline

### Unified Inbox
- AI Chatbot conversations, Tickets, Live Chat (Pro), Social Messages (Pro), Social Comments (Pro), Archived conversations
- Per-type notification counts, in-app notifications, business hours support, chat review and feedback form

### Upgraded AI Chatbot
- Enhanced AI reasoning and improved chat response generator
- Refined data source tabs: File, Post, Products, QnA, Text, URL
- Tone and language behavior settings; test chatbot with preview launcher mode
- Post and page meta box for training knowledge base directly from the WordPress editor

### Marketing Automation & Email Campaigns (Pro)
- Email templates with editor and library, bulk campaigns, email sequences for automated follow-up
- Failed email tracking and unsubscribe page

### Conversion Automation
- Promo Bar and Sales Notifications (1 free template each)
- Coupon Delivery automation (Pro), Proactive Sales automation (Pro)

### Social Media Integrations (Pro)
- Facebook Messages and Comments, Instagram Messages and Comments, WhatsApp
- Social lead campaign builder, social message processor, auto DM and comment automation

### Appointments, Tasks, and Scheduling (Pro)
- Task Management: Kanban board, list view, custom fields, role-based assignments
- Appointments: Public scheduling page, booking form inside chat widget, smart scheduling module

### CRM Analytics and Team Management (Pro)
- Reports for Contacts, Leads, Emails, Tasks, Tickets, Team performance
- Five predefined roles: Admin, Manager, Live Chat Agent, Salesperson, Marketer

## Why It Matters

Most WordPress businesses juggle separate CRM, chatbot, email automation, live chat, social messaging, and WooCommerce CRM tools. This creates recurring costs, data silos, and operational inefficiency. Helpmate 2.0.0 consolidates everything—AI chatbot, CRM, unified inbox, marketing automation, conversion tools, analytics, and scheduling—inside WordPress.

For WooCommerce stores, it becomes a complete customer lifecycle platform. For service-based businesses, it becomes a lead, booking, and automation hub. Helpmate positions WordPress as more than a CMS—it becomes a growth operating system.
